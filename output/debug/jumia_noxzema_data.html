Noxzema l Deep Cleansing Cream gives you clean, smooth skin with the Noxzema classic tingle. Enjoy the same 12 ounces of invigorating Noxzema, now in a jar with 45 percent less plastic than before.

This cream reaches deep into pores, removing oil, dirt, and makeup to leave your skin soft without over drying.
Leaves Skin Soft, Clean, and Smooth

Noxzema  Deep Cleansing Cream reaches deep into pores to remove dirt, oil, and makeup, leaving your skin soft and smooth without over drying. Dermatologist tested, the Deep Cleansing Cream refreshes with the classic Noxzema tingle.

Cooling Eucalyptus and Moisturizing Ingredients

Made with eucalyptus oil and soothing linseed and soybean oils, Noxzema  Cleansing Cream removes dirt and oils as it moisturizes.

Use Wet or Dry

The Deep Cleansing Cream can be used on a wet or dry face. Apply generously, and then use your fingertips or a wet washcloth to massage the cream into your skin using a circular motion. Rinse thoroughly, avoiding contact with your eyes. In case of eye contact, flush thoroughly with water.
